Senior ML Engineer — Acme Corp

Acme Corp is hiring a Senior Machine Learning Engineer to join our applied AI
team. This is a full-time, fully remote position open to candidates anywhere
in the US.

You will design and ship production machine learning systems that power our
recommendation and forecasting products, working closely with data engineering
and product teams. We are looking for someone who can take a model from a
notebook prototype to a reliable, monitored service running at scale.

Responsibilities:
- Build and maintain training and inference pipelines
- Own model performance in production, including drift monitoring
- Collaborate with backend engineers to deploy models behind low-latency APIs
- Mentor junior engineers on ML best practices

Requirements:
- 5+ years of professional experience building ML systems
- Strong Python skills and deep familiarity with PyTorch
- Hands-on experience deploying models on AWS
- Comfortable working independently in a remote-first team

Compensation: $180,000–$220,000 base, plus equity and full benefits.

Apply with your resume and a short note about a production ML system you have
shipped end to end.
